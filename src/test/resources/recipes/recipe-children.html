Ingredients

  • 2 T vegetable oil
  • 1/2 c chicken cubed
  • 1/4 c carrots diced
  • 1/4 c celery diced
  • 1/3 c onions diced
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 c chicken broth

Directions

  1. In large heavy bottomed pot heat oil on medium heat. Add chicken to pot and cook until browned. Remove chicken as it is finishes cooking.

  2. Once all chicken is cooked and removed from pot, add onions. Cook onions until they become translucent. About 5 - 7 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another 2 - 3 minutes.

  3. Add carrots and celery. Cook for 4 - 5 minutes, the celery should be barely translucent.

  4. Add chicken broth to the pot. Scrap any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes.

  5. Add salt and pepper to taste. You can also add parsley for a garnish.
